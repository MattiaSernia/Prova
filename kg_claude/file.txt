Call for tenders – City of Belval
Implementation of an artificial intelligence solution of assistance to the agents
City of Belval (120,000 inhabitants)
1. Context and objectives of the project
In the framework of its digital transformation strategy, the City of Belval wishes to improve the efficiency of its administrative services and the quality of service rendered to the users.
The municipal agents are today confronted with a growing complexity of the procedures, with a multiplicity of the sources of information (regulations, internal procedures, business systems) and with an increase of the volume of requests to process.
In this context, the local authority wishes to study and deploy an artificial intelligence solution allowing to assist the agents in their daily missions, notably to facilitate the access to the information, improve the quality of the answers and reduce the times of processing.
The present contract has for object the design, the implementation and the deployment of such a solution.
2. General description of the need
The expected solution will have to allow to provide to the municipal agents an intelligent assistance tool, accessible from their work environment, capable of accompanying them in their administrative activities.
This assistance could notably bear on:
the search for information in the internal procedures and the applicable regulation,
the help with the drafting of letters or of answers to the users,
the accompaniment in the instruction of files (town planning, social aids, civil status, etc.),
the suggestion of actions or of steps to realize in the framework of an administrative process.
The solution will have to integrate itself into the existing practices of the agents and not constitute a rupture in their modes of work.
3. Functional scope
The proposed solution will have to cover the following functionalities:
3.1 Assistance with the consultation of information
The solution will have to allow the agents to access quickly to reliable and up-to-date information coming from:
the internal procedures of the local authority,
the applicable regulatory texts,
the internal documents (guides, notes, repositories).
It will have to be in measure to contextualize the answers in function of the demand of the agent.
3.2 Assistance with the drafting
The solution will have to propose a help with the drafting of administrative contents, notably:
answers to the users,
administrative letters,
syntheses of files.
The generated contents will have to be adapted to the administrative context and compliant with the requirements of quality expected.
3.3 Assistance with the instruction of files
The solution will have to be in measure to assist the agents in the analysis and the processing of files, by:
proposing relevant information,
recalling the applicable rules,
suggesting actions or verifications to carry out.
It will have to take into account the context of the file when this one is available.
3.4 Collaboration and continuity of processing
The solution will have to facilitate the continuity of the processing of the files between agents and services, by allowing:
a sharing of relevant information,
an understanding of the context of an ongoing file,
an effective resumption of the processing by another agent.
4. Regulatory and legal constraints
The solution will have to imperatively respect the regulatory requirements in force.
4.1 Protection of data
The holder will have to guarantee a strict compliance to the General Regulation on the Protection of Data (GDPR).
To this title:
the data will have to be hosted exclusively on the territory of the European Union,
no transfer of data outside the EU will be authorized,
the sensitive data will have to be protected in an appropriate manner (encryption, anonymization if necessary).
4.2 Explicability and transparency
The local authority expects that the recommendations or proposals issued by the solution can be explained.
It will have to be possible, a posteriori, to understand the elements having led to an answer or a suggestion.
4.3 Responsibility
The solution will have to be designed as a tool of assistance.
No decision will be able to be automated without explicit validation of an agent.
5. Technical constraints
5.1 Performance
The solution will have to provide times of response compatible with a daily use by the agents.
To indicative title, a time of response lower than 2 seconds is expected for the current requests.
A high level of relevance of the answers is expected.
5.2 Availability
The solution will have to guarantee a high level of availability, compatible with the needs of the municipal services.
An objective of availability of the order of 99.9% is expected.
5.3 Integration
The solution will have to integrate itself with the existing system of information, notably:
the business softwares (civil status, town planning),
the electronic management of documents (EDM),
the user directories.
Interfaces of API type will be expected.
6. Sovereignty constraints
The local authority grants a particular importance to the sovereignty of the data.
To this title:
the hosting will have to be realized on an infrastructure of SecNumCloud type or equivalent,
a particular attention will be brought to the dependence vis-à-vis non-European suppliers.
7. Budgetary constraints
The global budget allocated to the project is estimated at 3 million euros over a duration of 4 years.
The annual cost of exploitation will have to remain mastered and compatible with the budgetary capacities of the local authority.
The holder will have to propose an approach allowing to optimize the costs while guaranteeing the quality of service expected.
8. Environmental constraints
In the framework of its policy of sustainable development, the City of Belval wishes to limit the environmental footprint of its systems of information.
The holder will have to propose a solution contributing to the reduction of the energetic consumption and relying, as much as possible, on infrastructures with low carbon impact.
9. Modalities of deployment
The project will have to be conducted in a progressive manner, with:
a pilot phase on a limited scope,
a progressive generalization to the entirety of the services.
An accompaniment to the change and actions of training of the agents will be expected.
10. Criteria of evaluation of the offers
The offers will be evaluated with regard to the following criteria:
quality and relevance of the proposed solution,
compliance to the regulatory requirements,
level of security,
global cost of the solution,
taking into account of the environmental issues,
capacity of innovation.


SATISFIED REQUIREMENTS
REQ-01: City of Belval improve efficiency of administrative services and quality of service to users (priority: MUST) (category: Digital Transformation)
REQ-02: AI solution assist agents in their daily missions (priority: MUST) (category: Agent Assistance)
REQ-03: AI solution facilitate access to information for municipal agents (priority: MUST) (category: Information Access)
REQ-04: AI solution improve quality of answers provided to users (priority: MUST) (category: Service Quality)
REQ-05: AI solution reduce processing times for requests (priority: MUST) (category: Efficiency)
REQ-06: solution provide intelligent assistance tool accessible from agents' work environment (priority: MUST) (category: Agent Assistance)
REQ-07: solution enable agents to search for information in internal procedures and applicable regulation (priority: MUST) (category: Information Search)
REQ-08: solution help agents with drafting of letters or answers to users (priority: MUST) (category: Drafting)
REQ-09: solution accompany agents in the instruction of files (town planning, social aids, civil status) (priority: MUST) (category: File Processing)
REQ-10: solution suggest actions or steps to realize in the framework of an administrative process (priority: MUST) (category: Process Support)
REQ-11: solution allow agents to access quickly reliable and up-to-date information from internal procedures, regulatory texts, and internal documents (priority: MUST) (category: Information Consultation)
REQ-12: solution contextualize answers in function of the demand of the agent (priority: MUST) (category: Information Consultation)
REQ-13: solution propose help with drafting of answers to users, administrative letters, and syntheses of files (priority: MUST) (category: Drafting)
REQ-14: solution assist agents in analysis and processing of files by proposing relevant information (priority: MUST) (category: File Instruction)
REQ-16: solution suggest actions or verifications to carry out on files (priority: MUST) (category: File Instruction)

SATISFIED CONSTRAINTS
CON-01: holder must guarantee strict compliance with GDPR (Regulatory)
CON-02: data must be hosted exclusively on the territory of the European Union (Sovereignty)
CON-03: data transfer outside the EU is not authorized (Sovereignty)
CON-04: sensitive data must be protected with encryption, anonymization if necessary (Technical)
CON-05: recommendations or proposals must be explainable a posteriori (Regulatory)
CON-06: no decision shall be automated without explicit validation of an agent (Regulatory)
CON-09: solution must integrate with business softwares (civil status, town planning) (Technical)
CON-10: solution must integrate with electronic document management system (EDM) (Technical)
CON-11: solution must integrate with user directories (Technical)
CON-12: interfaces must be of type API (Technical)
CON-13: hosting must be realized on infrastructure of SecNumCloud type or equivalent (Sovereignty)
CON-14: particular attention must be brought to dependence vis-à-vis non-European suppliers (Sovereignty)
CON-15: global budget is limited to 3 million euros over 4 years (Budgetary)
CON-16: annual exploitation cost must remain controlled and compatible with budgetary capacities (Budgetary)
CON-17: solution must contribute to reduction of energetic consumption (Environmental)
CON-18: solution must rely on infrastructures with low carbon impact (Environmental)
CON-19: solution must integrate into existing practices of agents without constituting a rupture (Technical)
